# AIFT Genesis Constitution

Status: canonical draft

## Preamble

AIFT Genesis exists to preserve the source pattern of the AI Freedom Trust Federation.

It is the constitutional genome from which trusts, repositories, applications, AI agents, maps, missions, economies, and communities may be instantiated.

## Article 1: Holographic Federation

The whole Federation pattern shall be reproducible inside every local trust seed.

Each local trust may contain identity, values, governance, Tree of Life, Living Atlas context, economy, AI steward, manifests, and Federation Link while retaining sovereignty over its own data and disclosure choices.

## Article 2: Local-First Sovereignty

A trust should be able to exist locally before it exists publicly.

Local folders, Git repositories, Markdown files, JSON manifests, and local-first application workspaces are constitutional design targets.

Federation synchronization is optional and permissioned.

## Article 3: Human Agency

AI agents serve as stewards and assistants. They do not replace human consent, responsibility, judgment, or lawful authority.

## Article 4: Privacy by Default

Private data remains local unless a trust explicitly chooses to share it.

Public, shared, private, and agent-local data must remain distinguishable.

## Article 5: Evidence-Bounded Claims

The Federation must distinguish doctrine, speculation, prototype, production, empirical evidence, legal claims, financial claims, and regulated activity.

Claims must not be strengthened beyond available evidence.

## Article 6: Forkability and Repair

Trusts, repositories, templates, and doctrine should be exportable, forkable, repairable, and inspectable wherever practical.

## Article 7: Productive Value

The Federation economy should reward contribution, repair, education, stewardship, useful work, and public goods rather than empty speculation or status games.

## Article 8: Living Atlas

Every user may be centered in their own interface frame while preserving the dignity and sovereignty of every other node.

## Article 9: Tree of Life

Every trust may grow as a Tree of Life: seed, roots, trunk, branches, leaves, fruit, and new seeds.

## Article 10: Genesis Authority

This repository defines the canonical starting pattern. Local trusts may inherit, adapt, fork, and extend it according to their own lawful governance and consent boundaries.
