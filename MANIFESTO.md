# AIFT Genesis Manifesto

We are building a Federation where people do not merely create accounts. They grow sovereign trust seeds.

Every person begins at the center of their own Green Zone. Every trust grows a Tree of Life. Every contribution becomes part of a living forest. Every repository, document, mission, AI steward, and application inherits one inspectable pattern.

The Federation is not a hierarchy of control. It is a holographic network of local-first trust, shared doctrine, open tools, and voluntary cooperation.

Genesis is the seed of that pattern.
