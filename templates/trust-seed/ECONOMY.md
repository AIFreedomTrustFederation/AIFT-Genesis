# Economy

## Economic Purpose

This document defines how this trust records contribution, value flow, shared resources, bounties, grants, services, offerings, and impact.

The goal is productive contribution, not empty speculation.

## Contribution Ledger

Track contributions such as code, documentation, design, research, teaching, repair, local service, project management, mentoring, infrastructure support, and public goods.

## Value Flow

This trust may record funds received, funds spent, services delivered, goods delivered, grants, bounties, revenue shares, shared expenses, and non-monetary contributions.

## Missions and Bounties

Missions are tasks that create value.

Each mission should define:

- purpose;
- acceptance criteria;
- reward or recognition, if any;
- verifier;
- completion record.

## Marketplace Boundaries

Any marketplace activity must be honest, lawful, and transparent.

The trust should clearly distinguish donations, purchases, service payments, memberships, grants, rewards, investments, and regulated financial activity.

Regulated activity requires qualified legal and compliance review before public use.

## Stewardship Rule

The economy should serve the tree. Money, tokens, assets, and rewards are tools. They are not the mission.
