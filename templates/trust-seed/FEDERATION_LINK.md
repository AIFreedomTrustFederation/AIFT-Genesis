# Federation Link

## Purpose

The Federation Link defines what this trust chooses to reveal, synchronize, publish, verify, or share with the wider AI Freedom Trust Federation.

## Default Rule

Private by default. Federation by consent.

## Link Status

- Enabled: `false`
- Public node: `false`
- Sync policy: explicit consent only

## Shareable Public Fields

This trust may optionally publish:

- public profile;
- public mission;
- public projects;
- public repositories;
- public contributions;
- public missions or bounties;
- public marketplace listings where legally permitted;
- public research or doctrine.

## Never Share Without Review

- precise private location;
- legal identity details;
- medical, financial, custody, or safety-sensitive information;
- private correspondence;
- private family or business records;
- regulated economic claims.
